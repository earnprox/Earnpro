Privacy Policy

Welcome to EarnproX. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information such as your Name, Phone Number (for Google OTP), and UPI ID to process payouts. We also collect screenshot proofs of tasks submitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity via Google Firebase Auth.
  • To process task rewards and transfer payouts via Cashfree.
  • To maintain ledger history and prevent fraud.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party gateways like Cashfree and Google Cloud. Your data is strictly shared only for processing payouts and security verifications.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
